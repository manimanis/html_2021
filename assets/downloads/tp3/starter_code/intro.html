Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is available in three distinct game mode versions: 1st Fortnite: Save the World, a cooperative shooter-survival game for up to four players to fight off zombie-like creatures and defend objects with fortifications they can build; 2nd Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play battle royale game where up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing; and 3rd Fortnite Creative, where players are given complete freedom to create worlds and battle arenas.
